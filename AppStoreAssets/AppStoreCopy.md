## Description (4000 characters max)

Transform how you engage with books. BookTalk lets you capture thoughts, quotes, and insights as you read — using your voice, camera, or keyboard.

**VOICE ANNOTATIONS**
Record audio notes hands-free while reading. Your recordings are automatically transcribed using on-device speech recognition — no internet required, completely private.

**PUSH TO TALK**
Enable Push to Talk for the ultimate hands-free experience. Record annotations from your Lock Screen, Dynamic Island, or Control Center without ever opening the app. Perfect for audiobook listeners or reading on the go.

**IMAGE ANNOTATIONS**
Snap photos of passages, diagrams, or interesting pages. Add captions to provide context for future reference.

**TEXT NOTES**
Type quick thoughts, favorite quotes, or reflections directly. Add page numbers to any annotation so you can find it again.

**SMART LIBRARY**
- Add books manually or scan ISBN barcodes for automatic title, author, and cover lookup
- Organize with archive functionality
- Each book has its own annotation timeline

**POWERFUL SEARCH**
Find any annotation instantly with full-text search across all your transcriptions and captions. Never lose a brilliant insight again.

**FEED VIEW**
Browse all your annotations across all books in one place. Shuffle for serendipitous rediscovery of past reading notes.

**PRIVACY FIRST**
- All data stored locally on your device
- Transcription happens on-device using Apple's Speech framework
- No accounts required
- No cloud sync, no tracking

**FEATURES AT A GLANCE**
• Voice recording with automatic transcription
• Push to Talk for hands-free recording
• Photo annotations with captions
• Text notes with page numbers
• ISBN barcode scanning
• Full-text search
• Shuffle feed for random discovery
• Archive books you've finished
• Export via Files app

Built for readers who want to remember what they read.

# App Review Notes

This app uses the following permissions:
- Microphone: For recording voice annotations
- Camera: For scanning book ISBN barcodes and taking photo annotations
- Photo Library: For adding existing photos as annotations
- Speech Recognition: For on-device transcription of voice recordings (no network required)
- Push to Talk: For recording voice annotations from Lock Screen/Dynamic Island

All processing happens on-device. No user data is transmitted to external servers.

Test account: Not required (no login needed)
