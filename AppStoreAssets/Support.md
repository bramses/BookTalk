# BookTalk Support

## Getting Started

### Adding Your First Book
1. Open BookTalk and tap the **+** button in the Library
2. Enter the book title and author manually, **or**
3. Tap **Scan Barcode** to scan the book's ISBN - title, author, and cover will be fetched automatically

### Creating Annotations
Open a book from your Library to start adding annotations:

- **Voice Notes**: Tap and hold the microphone button to record. Release to stop. Your recording is automatically transcribed.
- **Photos/Videos**: Tap the Media button to take a photo or choose from your library
- **Text Notes**: Tap the Note button to type a quick thought or quote

All annotations can include an optional page number for reference.

## Features

### Push to Talk (PTT)

2. Select **Push to Talk**
3. Tap **Join Channel**
4. Use the PTT button in Dynamic Island, Lock Screen, or Control Center to record

To leave PTT, tap the floating indicator at the bottom of the screen.

### Search
Find any annotation instantly using full-text search across all your transcriptions and captions. Tap the Search tab and start typing.

### Feed
Browse all annotations across all books in random order. Pull down to refresh and see a different mix.

### Archiving Books
Long-press a book cover and select **Archive** to move it out of your main library. Archived books can be viewed or restored from the menu.

### Spotlight Search
Your annotations are indexed for iOS Spotlight search. Search from your home screen to find annotations without opening the app.

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone not working
1. Go to **Settings > BookTalk** and ensure Microphone access is enabled
2. Make sure no other app is using the microphone
3. Try restarting the app

### Transcription not appearing
- Transcription happens on-device and may take a few seconds
- Ensure Speech Recognition is enabled in **Settings > BookTalk**
- Clear audio with minimal background noise transcribes best

### Camera/Photos not working
1. Go to **Settings > BookTalk**
2. Enable Camera access (for taking photos and scanning barcodes)
3. Enable Photo Library access (for choosing existing photos/videos)

### Push to Talk not working
- PTT requires iOS 16 or later
- Ensure you've joined a channel for a specific book
- Check that PTT is enabled in **Settings > BookTalk**
- Try leaving and rejoining the channel

### Barcode scanning fails
- Ensure good lighting and hold steady
- The ISBN barcode is usually on the back cover
- If scanning fails, you can always enter the book details manually

### Spotlight search not finding annotations
1. Go to **About & How to Use** in the app menu
2. Scroll to **Spotlight Search**
3. Tap **Reindex All Annotations**

## Data & Privacy

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

### Can I backup my data?
Yes. BookTalk's data is included in your iCloud or iTunes backups. You can also access the Documents folder via the Files app.

### Is transcription private?
Yes. Speech recognition runs entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework. No audio or text is sent to external servers.

### What data does the app collect?
None. BookTalk has no analytics, no accounts, and no network communication except for fetching book metadata when you scan an ISBN.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I sync between devices?**
A: Not currently. All data is stored locally on each device.

**Q: What formats are supported for audio?**
A: Recordings are saved as M4A (AAC) files.

**Q: Can I export my annotations?**
A: Your data is accessible via the Files app. Audio, images, and videos are stored in the Documents folder.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many books or annotations I can have?**
A: No artificial limits. Storage depends on your device capacity.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Yes, completely. The only feature requiring internet is ISBN lookup when adding books.
